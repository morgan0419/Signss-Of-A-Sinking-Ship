Mission Statement
Mental Health in teenagers is an important topic that is all but discussed. Instead it is brushed away as something for the "weak" or the "dramatic". However mental health is a real thing, and it is a part of all of us-whether you realize it or not. We presented "Signs of a Sinking Ship" because it's about time we give mental health its long overdue discussion.
