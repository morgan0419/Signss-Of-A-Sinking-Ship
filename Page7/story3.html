To Sleep or NOT to Sleep

I am constantly tired. Everyday I wake up with bags under my eyes. Everyday I am falling asleep in another class. Everyday I act out because of it. I can’t seem to control my emotions and this is all due to the lack of sleep I never receive. Just the other night for instance I was up until 5am reading up on my assignment, and although I admit I’m a huge procrastinator...this time was not my fault. My teacher literally assigned me 5 assignments over one night. This is something that happens on the daily. So, I force myself to stay up at all costs.

I can’t miss anymore assignments. If I do, I’m going to fail my class. If I fail this class, there’s no way any college is going to want me as their student.I need to go to a good college in order to get a good job. Although these homework assignments may not seem like they’re going to make an effect in the long run, they are. In order to keep up with this tremendous workload, I always drink coffee and even take Modafinil, a pill that allows me to stay uo and finish my work.

I know I shouldn’t take these pills, but it is very useful for me. A lot of my friends do it as well. We all are able to keep on top of our work that way. It is seen as the “norm” at my high school. It’s 3 a.m. right now. Without these pills, how will I get through the school day? I need them in order to thrive as a student.
